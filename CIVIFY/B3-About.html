About Civify Portal

Civify is a unified digital platform designed to empower the citizens of Bangladesh. Our goal is to bridge the gap between the government and the people by providing transparent, fast, and secure digital services.

Our Mission

To digitize administrative tasks and ensure every citizen's voice is heard through an efficient reporting system.

Our Vision

A "Smart Bangladesh" where technology simplifies governance and enhances public welfare at every level.

Key Objectives:

  • Instant issue reporting and tracking.
  • Direct communication with Ward representatives.
  • Secure digital identification for all users.
